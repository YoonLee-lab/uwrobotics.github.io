With its initial launch in 2017, the UWRT took on this outreach program to educate middle/high school students about the expanding field of robotics, technology and STEM. The goal of the program is to inspire KW youth to explore the various possibilities in the aforementioned fields and hopefully ignite an innovative spark in the minds of the young audience.
