About Us

Since our inception in the previous decade, we've participated in many competitions and built various robots. We have two simple goals. Our first goal is to explore the field of robotics, applying the theoretical knowledge gained in classrooms to real life. Our second goal is to share our love of robotics with the world, which we achieve through outreach programs and the University of Waterloo's annual autonomous robotics competition.
