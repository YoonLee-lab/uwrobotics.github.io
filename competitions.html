Mars Rover Competition (URC)

Organized by the Mars Society, the annual University Rover Challenge takes applications from over 90 teams, ultimately hosting a competition of 30 teams from around the world.

The University of Waterloo has performed exceptionally well in previous years, placing 22nd in 2018 and 15th in 2017. Join us in completely redesigning our rover for the 2020 Utah-based competition.

International Autonomous Robot Racing Competition (IARRC)

IARRC is an annual autonomous mobile robotics challenge hosted by the University of Waterloo, as part of our outreach initiative to share our passion of robotics. Teams from Canada, United States, India, Thailand, and other countries participate in the competition, making it truly international. In 2017, the University of Waterloo placed first, while in 2018 the team placed fifth. The competition continues to grow with a revamp on the way this year.
