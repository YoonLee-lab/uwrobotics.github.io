Mars Rover Competition

Organized by the Mars Society, the annual University Rover Challenge hosts over 96 teams all across the globe. The goal is to test the performance of each team's robot in a simulated Mars environment.

The University of Waterloo performed exceptionally well in 2017, placing 22nd globally out of the 96 teams that applied! Join us in improving and re-designing our rover for the 2019 Utah-based competition.

International Autonomous Robot Racing Competition (IARRC)

IARRC is an annual autonomous mobile robotics challenge hosted by the University of Waterloo, as part of our outreach initiative to share our passion of robotics. Teams from Canada, United States, India, Thailand, and other countries participate in the competition, making it truly international. In 2017, the University of Waterloo placed first. This year, we'll be doing a major overhaul of the entire system; stay tuned!
