Mars Rover Competition

Hosted by the Mars Society, the annual University Rover Challenge, hosts over 60 teams from all over the world that compete with their rovers. The goal of the competition is to see which team's rover best mimicks the requirements of a rover actually used on Mars.

The Waterloo team placed 3rd in Canada, and 15th internationally in 2017! Join us in improving and re-designing our rover for the 2018 competition in Utah.
